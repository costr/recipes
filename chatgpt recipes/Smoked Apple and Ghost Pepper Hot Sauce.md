# Smoked Apple and Smoked Ghost Pepper Flakes Hot Sauce Recipe

## Ingredients
- 1 cup of smoked apple, finely diced
- 1/2 cup of distilled white vinegar
- 1/4 cup of smoked ghost pepper flakes
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon of honey
- 1/2 teaspoon of salt
- 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper

## Instructions
1. In a medium saucepan, combine the smoked apple, distilled white vinegar, smoked ghost pepper flakes, minced garlic, honey, salt, and ground black pepper. 
2. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce the heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the sauce thickens.
3. Remove the saucepan from heat and let it cool for about 10 minutes.
4. Blend the sauce in a blender or food processor until smooth.
5. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.
6. Allow the sauce to cool completely before storing in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.

This hot sauce is perfect for adding a smoky and spicy flavor to any dish. The smoked apple and smoked ghost pepper flakes give a unique and delicious taste to the sauce, and the honey balances out the heat. Feel free to adjust the amount of ghost pepper flakes to your liking. Enjoy!
