# Easy Pickled Red Onions

These quick and easy pickled red onions are a versatile condiment that adds a bright pop of color and a tangy crunch to tacos, salads, burgers, and bowls.

 * Prep Time: 10 mins
 * Total Time: 40 mins
 * Servings: 16 (approx. 2 cups)

## Ingredients

 * 1 cup water
 * ½ cup distilled white vinegar
 * ½ cup apple cider vinegar
 * 1 ½ tablespoons sugar
 * 1 ½ teaspoons salt
 * 1 large red onion, thinly sliced (about 2 cups)

## Instructions

 * Prepare the Brine: Whisk water, distilled white vinegar, apple cider vinegar, sugar, and salt together in a medium bowl until the sugar and salt are completely dissolved.
 * Pack the Jar: Place the sliced red onions into a clean pint-sized (2-cup) glass jar.
 * Submerge the Onions: Pour the vinegar mixture over the onions. If necessary, use a spoon to press the onions down so they are fully submerged in the liquid.
 * Let it Set: Let the mixture stand at room temperature for at least 30 minutes.
 * Store: Cover and refrigerate. The onions are ready to eat after 30 minutes but will continue to develop flavor and turn a bright pink color the longer they sit.
Tips for Success
 * Thin Slices: For the best texture, use a mandoline to get uniform, paper-thin slices.
 * Variations: Add a clove of garlic, a few peppercorns, or a pinch of red pepper flakes to the jar for extra flavor.
 * Storage: Keep refrigerated in an airtight container for up to 2 weeks.
